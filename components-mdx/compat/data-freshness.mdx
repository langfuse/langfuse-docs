**Data availability:** Data from older SDKs (`langfuse-python` < `4.0.0`, `langfuse-js` < `5.0.0`) or direct OpenTelemetry exporters that do not send `x-langfuse-ingestion-version: 4` can be delayed by up to 10 minutes on v2 endpoints. Upgrade to [Python SDK v4.7.0+](/docs/observability/sdk/upgrade-path/python-v3-to-v4) or [JS/TS SDK v5.4.0+](/docs/observability/sdk/upgrade-path/js-v4-to-v5), or [set that header on your OTEL span exporter](/integrations/native/opentelemetry#real-time-ingestion) to see new data in real time. Details: [Versions & Compatibility](/docs/compatibility#faq-delay).

The [migration guide](/docs/deprecated-api-migration) is the canonical per-endpoint mapping with parameter tables and before/after examples; the old endpoints stay documented in the [Deprecated APIs reference](/docs/api-and-data-platform/features/deprecated-apis#endpoints).

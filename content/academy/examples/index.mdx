# Examples

The academy describes the AI engineering loop in general terms. These examples show how it plays out for specific applications: how the team enters the loop, which signals drive improvement, and how the setup matures over time.

- [Customer support chatbot](/academy/examples/customer-support-chatbot): a high-risk, customer-facing agent rolled out in phases, from offline development on historical tickets to answering customers directly.
- [Music streaming DJ](/academy/examples/music-streaming-dj): a net-new, low-risk beta feature that goes live almost immediately and improves from listening behavior.

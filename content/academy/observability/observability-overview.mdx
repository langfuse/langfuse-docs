# LLM Observability

> I'd like to see what my agent is doing

This section dives into LLM observability. It's the first step towards a solid AI setup. Even if you don't feel ready for other aspects of LLM engineering, having traces of your agents is always a good idea. You can study their behavior, make small fixes based on what you see, and form a more grounded opinion on what good looks like for your agent in specific situations.

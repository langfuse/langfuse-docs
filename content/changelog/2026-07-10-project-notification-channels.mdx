Langfuse can now send failure notifications to **Slack** or a **webhook**, in addition to admin emails:

- A scheduled blob storage export fails.
- Langfuse deactivates an evaluator after an unrecoverable error, such as a deleted model or LLM connection.

Open **Project Settings → Notifications** to add one project notification channel.

# How to invite co-workers to your Langfuse Project

## How do I invite someone to a Langfuse Project?

Head to your Project's settings and click the 'add a new member' button under the heading 'Project Members'. Enter the email address that your co-workers use in Langfuse. You can find more information about adding users to a project [here](/docs/rbac#add-users-to-a-project).

## I cannot join a Langfuse project

### I was invited but did not receive an invitation email

Langfuse associates membership with your email address. If the email you were invited to matches an existing Langfuse account on the **same data region**, the project simply appears in your dashboard the next time you sign in — there is no separate invitation email to click. This is the most common reason an "invite" looks like it never arrived.

A short checklist:

1. **Sign in (or sign up) with the exact email that was invited**, on the same data region (EU, US, Japan, or HIPAA — see below) where the inviter created the project. If you sign up with a different email — for example, via Google or GitHub SSO under a personal address — the invite will not match.
2. After signing in, **switch to the inviting organization** using the organization switcher in the top-left of the UI. The project will not appear until you select the correct organization.
3. If you signed up before being invited, you may need to **sign out and back in** so the new membership is picked up.
4. If the invite still does not appear, ask the inviter to remove and re-add your email under **Project settings → Members**, and double-check for typos in the email address.
5. Check that the email used for the invitation matches the email shown in the bottom-left profile menu of the Langfuse UI.

### Are you on the correct Langfuse tenant? (EU / US / Japan / HIPAA?)

Langfuse Cloud runs in multiple data regions: the [European Union](https://cloud.langfuse.com), the [USA](https://us.cloud.langfuse.com), [Japan](https://jp.cloud.langfuse.com), and a separate [HIPAA-compliant US region](https://hipaa.cloud.langfuse.com).
Make sure that you are logged into the data region that you have created your project in. You can select the data region during log-in. You can also see it in the URL bar (`cloud.langfuse.com` = EU, `us.cloud.langfuse.com` = US, `jp.cloud.langfuse.com` = Japan, `hipaa.cloud.langfuse.com` = HIPAA).

Find out more about [Langfuse Cloud Data Regions](/security/data-regions).

### Are you using the correct email address?

If you have been invited to a project, make sure you are logging in with the exact email address that this invite has gone out to. If you log in with SSO (e.g. GitHub or Google), your email address might be different from what you expected.

If you click your name in the bottom left of the Langfuse UI, you will see the email address we have on record for you.

### Are you the project owner?

If someone else in your organization created the project, ask them to re-invite you (to your Langfuse project).

## Access Roles in Langfuse

### What types of access can I grant to data in Langfuse?

The basic roles for granting access in Langfuse are: Owner, Admin, Member and Viewer. You can see the specific access rights granted to each role in the [Langfuse roles and rbac documentation](/docs/rbac#roles).

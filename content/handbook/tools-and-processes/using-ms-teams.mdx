# Using MS Teams

We offer customer support via MS Teams as an alternative to Slack. All MS Teams channels are synced to [Pylon](/handbook/support/using-pylon) alongside Slack channels.

## Customer Teams Channel

### 1. Create a new Team

Go to the [Teams and Channels overview](https://teams.cloud.microsoft/) and click **Create team**. Ask Jannik to get access to the Langfuse MS Teams account.

Use the following naming convention and settings:

- **Team name**: `ext-<company>-x-langfuse`
- **Description**: `Support channel with the Langfuse team`
- **Team type**: Private
- **First channel name**: `langfuse-support`

<Frame>
  ![Create Team](/images/handbook/using-ms-teams/create-team.png)
</Frame>

### 2. Add members

Add the following members to the team:

- **Pylon integration**: `pylon-integration-user@LangfuseSupport.onmicrosoft.com` (as Member)
- **Clemens**: `clemens@langfuse.com` (as Guest)
- Customer email addresses (as Guests)

<Frame>
  ![Add Members](/images/handbook/using-ms-teams/add-members.png)
</Frame>

### 3. Post a welcome message

Once the team is set up, post a welcome message in the `langfuse-support` channel to introduce yourself and let the customer know they can reach out here.

<Frame>
  ![Welcome Message](/images/handbook/using-ms-teams/welcome-message.png)
</Frame>

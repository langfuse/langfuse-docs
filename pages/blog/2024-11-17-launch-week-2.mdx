## Join us for Launch Week

We are excited to announce **Langfuse's second Launch Week**! Starting on **Sunday, November 17, 2024**, we'll unveil a major upgrade to the Langfuse platform **every day until Friday**.

We're focusing on expanding support for new model capabilities and enhancing how Langfuse integrates into your development workflow, empowering you to build more robust and versatile AI applications.

<Steps>

### GitHub
⭐️ [Star us](https://github.com/langfuse/langfuse/) on GitHub & see _all_ of our releases!
### Channels
[Twitter](https://x.com/langfuse) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/langfuse/) will be our main channels for all of Launch Week.
### Town Hall
Join our [virtual town hall](https://lu.ma/c7zsbc3b) on Wednesday.

</Steps>

## Launch Week Events

### Wednesday: Virtual Town Hall [#townhall]

You're invited to our [virtual town hall](https://lu.ma/c7zsbc3b) where we'll demo the new features and discuss how they integrate into your development workflow. We will also answer your questions and talk about the future of Langfuse.

- **When:** Wednesday, November 20, 2024, at 10 am PT / 7pm CET
- **Where:** https://lu.ma/c7zsbc3b

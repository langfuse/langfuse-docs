We are proud that Langfuse is the **most used open-source LLM Ops tool** out there. 

Langfuse offers developers a comprehensive set of tools for **observability**, **evaluation**, and **prompt management**, making it a good choice to develop and improve AI applications in production. We receive incredible feedback from our users, and it motivates us to continue building the **best product for the community**. 

We have grown from a few core users within YC to **thousands of teams who use Langfuse in startups and enterprises**. Your support and engagement drive us to innovate and improve every day. Thank you for being a part of our journey as we work together to shape the future of LLM Ops.

## Why chose Langfuse?

1. **Large community:** Langfuse has numerous actively maintained **integrations** with the latest LLM frameworks (such as [Langchain](docs/integrations/langchain), [LlamaIndex](docs/integrations/llamaindex), and [OpenAI](docs/integrations/openai)) and a helpful community on [Discord](https://discord.langfuse.com) and [GitHub](https://github.com/orgs/langfuse/discussions).
2. **Battle-tested:** Langfuse is used in production by thousands of users and has a proven track record of **reliability** and **performance**.

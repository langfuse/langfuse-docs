# Careers

Langfuse is building the leading **open-source LLM engineering platform**. We are based in Berlin, Germany and work [in person](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rawert_why-we-decided-to-build-langfuse-in-person-activity-7150905769528385536-yuqi).

We have raised a $4M seed round from Lightspeed, La Famiglia, Y Combinator ([announcement blog post](/blog/announcing-our-seed-round)).

## Open Positions

We are currently not hiring for any specific positions and expect to open up engineering positions throughout the year. We are only hiring in engineering for now.

If you are interested in working with us now or in the future, we would love to hear from you: ping us at careers@langfuse.com

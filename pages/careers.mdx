# Careers

Langfuse is building the leading **open-source LLM engineering platform**. We are based in Berlin, Germany and work [in person](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rawert_why-we-decided-to-build-langfuse-in-person-activity-7150905769528385536-yuqi).

We have raised a [$4M seed round](/blog/announcing-our-seed-round) from Lightspeed, La Famiglia, Y Combinator and angels.

Come join us and build scale one of the most exciting open source devtool companies in Europe.

## We are hiring

We are hiring for technical roles, including software engineering and developer relations. If you are excited about delivering exceptional developer experiences and building a high-quality [open-source](/docs/open-source) product alongside an insanely motivated team that [ships](/changelog), please reach out: careers@langfuse.com

## Open Position

- [Backend Engineer](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/langfuse/jobs/mnrdwla-backend-engineer)

- [Developer Advocate / Devrel](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/langfuse/jobs/uHysbKH-developer-advocate-devrel)

Please apply via Work At a Startup or send us an email with your CV to careers@langfuse.com

Note: You need to be located in Berlin, Germany or willing to relocate. Your superday interview will be held in-person in Berlin. The first few interviews will be conducted via Zoom.

Langfuse takes information security seriously and invests in the security, compliance, and privacy standards of its organization and managed cloud offering. You can find more information in our [Security & Privacy Center](/security).

As of now, Langfuse is compliant with the following frameworks:

- ISO 27001:2022
- SOC 2 Type II
- GDPR

If you are an enterprise and want to learn how Langfuse can help you meet your security and compliance requirements, review our [Enterprise FAQ](/enterprise) or contact us at enteprise@langfuse.com.

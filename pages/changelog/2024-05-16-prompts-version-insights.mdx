Langfuse lets you combine [Tracing](/docs/tracing) with [Prompt Management](/docs/prompts). Thereby you can get a sense for differences in metrics across versions of your prompts:

- Latency
- Cost
- Input/Output tokens
- Quality/Evaluations
- Generations count

Additionally, you can view the generations linked to each prompt version.

Check out the [Prompt Management docs](/docs/prompts/get-started#link-with-langfuse-tracing-optional) for instructions on how to link prompts to traces.

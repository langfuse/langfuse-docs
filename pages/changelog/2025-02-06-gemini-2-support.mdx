What's new in Gemini 2.0? From [Google release notes](https://blog.google/technology/google-deepmind/gemini-model-updates-february-2025/):

- Gemini 2.0 Pro: Advanced model with 2M token context window, optimized for coding and complex prompting tasks
- Gemini 2.0 Flash-Lite: Cost-effective variant matching 1.5's speed and cost while outperforming it on most benchmarks
- Gemini 2.0 Flash, general availability: Optimized for high-volume tasks with 1M token context window and multimodal reasoning capabilities

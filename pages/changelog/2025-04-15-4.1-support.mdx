Some highlights about GPT-4.1 from the [OpenAI release notes](https://openai.com/index/gpt-4-1):

- **Improved Performance**: GPT-4.1 surpasses GPT-4o in all areas, with major gains in coding (54.6% on SWE-bench Verified) and instruction following
- **Larger Context Windows**: Supports up to 1 million tokens of context with improved long-context comprehension
- **Refreshed Knowledge**: Features a refreshed knowledge cutoff of June 2024
- **Model Variety**: Available in three versions - GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, and GPT-4.1 nano - to optimize between performance and cost

Introducing **Pivot Tables** for Langfuse custom dashboards! Analyze your data across multiple dimensions simultaneously with this powerful new widget type.


## Perfect for Complex Questions

Answer sophisticated analytics questions like:
- How do quality scores vary by model and user?
- What's our cost breakdown by feature, model, and environment?
- How do latencies compare between prompts?

## Getting Started

1. Go to **Dashboards → Widgets**
2. Click **New Widget** → **Pivot Table**
3. Select your data source and select metrics and dimensions
4. Apply filters and save your view

Pivot tables work seamlessly with all existing dashboard features including real-time updates and team sharing.

Start experimenting with GPT-5.1 now in the [Langfuse LLM playground](/docs/playground), leverage it to power your [LLM-as-a-judge evaluations](/docs/scores/model-based-evals), and monitor usage with our comprehensive [cost tracking](/docs/model-usage-and-cost) capabilities.

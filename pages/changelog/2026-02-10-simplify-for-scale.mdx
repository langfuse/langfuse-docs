## How we did it

We rebuilt Langfuse around three core principles:

**Immutable by default**: Observations are written once and never modified. This eliminates expensive deduplication operations that slowed down queries.

**No joins required**: Trace-level attributes are propagated directly onto observations in the SDK, so queries run on a single table with no costly joins.

**Observations first**: Observations become the single source of truth, simplifying everything from APIs to UI.


## Upgrade to unlock the performance

- **Python SDK**: Version 3.0.0 or higher
- **JS/TS SDK**: Version 4.0.0 or higher

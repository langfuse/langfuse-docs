# How do I report an issue/feedback/bug?

**Feedback/Feature Requests**: Please raise feedback and feature requests through our [GitHub Discussions](/ideas).

**Bugs/Issues**: If you have encountered a bug, please raise it on our [GitHub Issues](/issues). Please include code/screenshots/context that will help us reproduce your issue.

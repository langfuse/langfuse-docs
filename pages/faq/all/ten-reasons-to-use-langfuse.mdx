# Ten Reasons to Use Langfuse

Langfuse is a powerful tool designed to enhance LLM observability, evaluations, and prompt management. 

## Here are ten reasons why you should consider using it:

1. **Open Source**: Langfuse is open source. You can customize and self-host it. You can trust for it to be around for the long-haul.

2. **Platform Agnostic**: Langfuse works seamlessly with any model and integrates with frameworks like Llama Index and LangChain.

3. **Detailed Metrics**: Langfuse provides in-depth insights and metrics to improve your LLMs.

4. **Real-time Monitoring**: Langfuse enables real-time monitoring to keep track of your models' performance.

5. **Flexible Deployment**: Whether on-premises or in the cloud, Langfuse adapts to your deployment needs.

6. **Scalable**: Built to scale with your projects, Langfuse handles projects of all sizes: from small to enterprise-level needs.

7. **Compliance Ready**: Langfuse is ISO27001 and SOC2 Type 2 certified as well as GDPR compliant.

8. **Customizable Dashboards**: Langfuse lets you create dashboards that suit your specific requirements.

9. **Easy Integration**: Langfuse is simple to integrate with existing workflows and tools.

10. **Community Support**: You will benefit from Langfuse's supportive open-source community and continuous updates.
